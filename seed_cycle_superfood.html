When do we Seed Cycle?

Seed cycling can be used at any stage of a woman’s life (including post-menopause). You can start as early as your first ever period cycle to avoid acne, irregular periods, PMS or even PCOS!

Days 1 to 14: Pepitas Flax Seed Cycle Superfood Start with Pepitas Flax Seed Cycle Superfood for 14 days during the first and second phases (menstrual & follicular phases), aka, first day of your menstrual bleed until ovulation
